Traditional machine learning models posed limitations in preserving data privacy, especially when dealing with sensitive financial information.Our SolutionWe proposed integrating Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) with the powerful XGBoost model to provide secure and privacy-preserving predictive analytics capabilities.
